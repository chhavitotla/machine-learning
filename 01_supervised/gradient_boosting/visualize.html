Gradient Boosting

One weak tree can only draw a step. But fit each new tree to whatever error is left, add it shrunk by ν, and the steps stack into the wave. Watch the residuals (middle panel) collapse toward zero — then push rounds and ν too far and watch the holdout error turn back up: the ensemble starts memorizing noise.
