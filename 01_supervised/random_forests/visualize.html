Each new tree trains on a bootstrap resample (watch the sampled points flash — bigger ring = drawn more times) with a random feature per split, so every tree overfits differently. Their jagged staircase boundaries stack up as faint white lines; the bold boundary is the majority vote, and it gets smoother and more right as the disagreements cancel.
