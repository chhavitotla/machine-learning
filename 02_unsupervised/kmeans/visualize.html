K-Means Clustering

No labels, no teacher — just assign every point to its nearest centroid, then update every centroid to the mean of its flock, forever, until nothing moves. Click the canvas to place points (or scatter blobs), drop K centroids, and watch the two phases alternate. The tinted territories are each centroid's Voronoi cell.
