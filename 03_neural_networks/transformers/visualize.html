softmax(QKᵀ/√dk)·V, one cell at a time. Click any token in the sentence to see where it looks. Line thickness = attention weight. The grid below is the full attention matrix: row = the token doing the looking (query), column = the token being looked at (key). Switch heads — each one has learned to track something different.

attention(i→j) = softmax over j of ( qi·kj / √dk ) — try the √dk toggle to see why scaling matters
Without the scaling, dot products are large, softmax goes one-hot, and the heatmap collapses to single bright cells — the exact failure the √dk prevents.
