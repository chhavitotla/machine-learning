The Loss Function Explorer

A loss function prices a mistake; its gradient is the push the model feels. MSE pushes harder the farther off you are, MAE pushes the same no matter what, Huber switches between them at δ, and cross-entropy prices confidence. Drag the prediction slider and watch the gradients diverge — then drag the outlier point below and watch which fitted line takes the bait.

Loss vs prediction, target y = 1 (dashed). BCE shown for label y = 1 over the raw score (through a sigmoid).
