K-Fold Cross-Validation

Sixty real samples, one real logistic regression. The strip is your dataset cut into K folds; hit Play and each fold takes its turn as held-out validation while the model trains live on the rest. Every fold lands one score bar — and after K of them you get the prize a single split can't give you: a mean and an error bar. Then toggle Single split and watch one-shot scores swing on identical data.
